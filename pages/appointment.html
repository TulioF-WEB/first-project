ASKED QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best way to schedule an appointment?

Please use the form at your left or contact us directly through our registered lines.

What are the recommendations for appointments?

Please try to always arrive at least 5 minutes prior to your schedule to make sure we can follow it properly.

How can i improve/change my room?

Please contact us directly at the reception with your documents in hand to proceed with any change.

How long does blood exams usually take to get ready?

Blood exams period may vary from 2-9 business days depending on the type of exam conducted.

Can i visit a patient who has undergone surgery recently?

Patients are allowed up to 4 visitors per day after a surgery, with time varying depending on the severity of the case.
